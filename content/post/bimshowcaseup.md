## [BIM Showcase](https://bimshowcase.romangolev.com/) application is live
Have you ever thought about creating a portal where you can share you BIM Models privately in a more creative way? The BIM Showcase web app does it. It streamlines BIM models throught APS(Forge) viewer or Cesuim viewer into your browser. This is the early version and it solely gets preloaded by me models and have a simple console for creating new users by admin.
